<p><ul><li>Solar Salt</li><li>Rock Salt</li><li>Vacuum Salt</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Encapsulated Salt market comprises three primary types: Solar Salt, Rock Salt, and Vacuum Salt. Solar Salt is produced through the evaporation of seawater, commonly used in food and industrial applications. Rock Salt is mined from underground deposits and is often used in de-icing and chemical processes. Vacuum Salt is generated by evaporating brine in a vacuum, ensuring high purity levels, and is widely used in food processing and pharmaceuticals. Each type serves distinct applications based on its properties and sourcing methods.</p></p>
