<p><p>The E-bike battery market comprises primarily two types: Lead Storage SLA (Sealed Lead Acid) and Cylindrical Lithium-ion cells. SLA batteries are cost-effective, heavier, and offer lower energy density, making them suitable for budget-friendly e-bikes. In contrast, cylindrical Lithium-ion cells provide a higher energy density, longer lifespan, and lighter weight, enhancing performance and range. As the e-bike industry grows, Lithium-ion batteries are increasingly favored for their efficiency and technological advancements, while SLA batteries continue to serve niche markets.</p></p>
