<p><p>Internal Developer Platforms (IDPs) can be categorized into two primary market types: cloud-based and local-based. Cloud-based IDPs are hosted on remote servers, offering scalability, ease of access, and minimal maintenance for organizations, ideal for teams with distributed members. In contrast, local-based IDPs are installed within a company's infrastructure, allowing for greater control over data and security. These platforms are suited for organizations that require strict compliance or have specific customization needs, leveraging their existing hardware resources.</p></p>
