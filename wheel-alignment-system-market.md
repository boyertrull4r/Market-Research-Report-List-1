<p><p>The wheel alignment system market comprises three main types: 3D wheel aligners, CCD wheel aligners, and laser wheel aligners. </p><p>3D wheel aligners utilize advanced imaging technology to provide precise alignment measurements from multiple angles. CCD wheel aligners employ Charge-Coupled Device sensors for accurate readings and easy setup. Laser wheel aligners, on the other hand, use laser beams to ensure alignment precision, offering quick and straightforward adjustments. Each system caters to specific needs in automotive repair and maintenance, enhancing vehicle safety and performance.</p></p>
