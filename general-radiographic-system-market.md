<p><p>The General Radiographic System market encompasses two primary types: X-ray radiographic systems and gamma-ray radiographic systems. X-ray radiographic systems utilize high-energy electromagnetic waves to create images of internal body structures, commonly employed in medical diagnostics and industrial applications. In contrast, gamma-ray radiographic systems use gamma radiation for imaging, often utilized in nuclear medicine and certain industrial testing scenarios. Both systems play crucial roles in non-invasive imaging, enabling precise diagnoses and assessments across healthcare and quality control sectors.</p></p>
