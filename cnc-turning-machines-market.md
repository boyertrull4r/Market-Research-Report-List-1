<p><p>The CNC turning machines market encompasses several key types. CNC turning lathes are used for shaping metal and other materials by rotating them against cutting tools. CNC vertical milling machines provide precision machining by moving tools vertically against a stationary workpiece. CNC flame and plasma cutting machines are designed for cutting thick materials using high-temperature processes. Lastly, CNC grinding machines specialize in finishing processes, particularly for turbine parts, ensuring high precision and surface quality. Each type serves specific manufacturing needs.</p></p>
