<p><p>The glass bead sterilizer market is primarily divided into two types: vertical and horizontal. Vertical glass bead sterilizers are designed to sterilize instruments by placing them vertically in the bead chamber, allowing for efficient use of space and optimized heat distribution. In contrast, horizontal types offer a wider chamber, enabling the sterilization of larger or multiple instruments simultaneously. Both types utilize high-temperature glass beads to ensure rapid and effective sterilization, catering to various medical and dental applications.</p></p>
