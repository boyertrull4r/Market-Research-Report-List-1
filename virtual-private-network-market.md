<p><p>The Virtual Private Network (VPN) market encompasses three primary types: Cloud, IP, and Multiprotocol Label Switching (MPLS). Cloud VPNs provide secure, scalable access over the internet, allowing users to connect from various devices without on-premises infrastructure. IP VPNs use Internet Protocol to securely connect multiple sites, beneficial for organizations with dispersed locations. In contrast, MPLS VPNs offer enhanced performance and reliability by prioritizing data traffic, making them suitable for businesses requiring robust and efficient network management.</p></p>
