<p><p>POS systems for restaurants can be categorized into two main types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based systems are hosted on remote servers, allowing real-time access to data from anywhere with an internet connection. They offer flexibility and scalability, ideal for multi-location businesses. On-premises systems are installed directly on the restaurant's hardware, providing complete control over data and operations. While they may require higher initial investment, they can be more reliable in environments with unstable internet connectivity.</p></p>
