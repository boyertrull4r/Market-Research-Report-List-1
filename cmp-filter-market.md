<p><p>The CMP (Chemical Mechanical Planarization) filter market includes two primary types: cartridge filters and capsule filters. Cartridge filters are large, replaceable units commonly used in semiconductor fabrication to remove particles and maintain fluid purity. In contrast, capsule filters are smaller, single-use systems that provide similar filtration but are optimized for smaller volumes and quicker processing. Both types play critical roles in ensuring contaminant-free environments, enhancing the efficiency and effectiveness of chemical mechanical planarization processes in advanced technology manufacturing.</p></p>
