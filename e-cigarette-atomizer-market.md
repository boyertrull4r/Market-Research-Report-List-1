<p><p>The e-cigarette atomizer market features two primary types: Rebuildable Drip Atomizers (RDA) and Rebuildable Tank Atomizers (RTA). RDAs allow users to manually build their coil and wick setups, providing a customizable vaping experience and enhanced flavor by dripping e-liquid directly onto the coils. In contrast, RTAs combine the benefits of a tank system with rebuildable components, allowing for larger e-liquid capacity while retaining user customization. Both types appeal to experienced vapers seeking personalized options and improved performance.</p></p>
