<p><p>Rotor concentrators are advanced air treatment systems designed to capture volatile organic compounds (VOCs) and other hazardous air pollutants from industrial exhaust streams. These systems utilize a rotating adsorption mechanism, allowing them to efficiently concentrate pollutants on specialized rotary adsorbent materials.
