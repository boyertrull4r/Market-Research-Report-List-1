<p><ul><li>Monolithic Cleaning Equipment</li><li>Tank Cleaning Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The silicon wafer cleaning equipment market comprises two primary types: monolithic cleaning equipment and tank cleaning equipment. Monolithic cleaning equipment offers integrated, compact solutions that streamline the cleaning process, ensuring high efficiency and reduced contamination risk. In contrast, tank cleaning equipment consists of larger, typically multi-tank systems that enhance the thoroughness of cleaning by allowing multiple wafers to be processed simultaneously, accommodating various cleaning chemicals. Both types are essential for maintaining the purity and quality of silicon wafers in semiconductor fabrication.</p></p>
