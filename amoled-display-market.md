<p><strong>The AMOLED Display Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active-Matrix Organic Light-Emitting Diode(AMOLED Display)</li><li>Passive Matrix OLED(PMOLED)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The AMOLED display market consists primarily of Active-Matrix Organic Light-Emitting Diode (AMOLED) and Passive Matrix OLED (PMOLED) technologies. AMOLED uses a grid of thin-film transistors to control individual pixels, providing superior image quality, faster response times, and lower power consumption, making it ideal for smartphones and televisions. In contrast, PMOLED features a simpler design with limited applications, often utilized in smaller displays like wearables or basic screens due to its lower cost and complexity. Each has distinct advantages catering to different market needs.</p></p>
