<p><p>The aluminum scaffolding market primarily consists of two types: single-width scaffolding and double-width scaffolding. Single-width scaffolding is narrower, typically used in confined spaces where access is limited, allowing for easier maneuverability. In contrast, double-width scaffolding offers greater platform space and stability, making it suitable for larger construction projects where multiple workers may need to operate simultaneously. Both types are lightweight, durable, and easy to assemble, catering to various industrial, commercial, and residential applications.</p></p>
