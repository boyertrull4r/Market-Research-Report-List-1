<p><p>Radial leads multilayer ceramic capacitors (MLCCs) are categorized based on their dielectric materials and temperature stability. NPO (COG) capacitors are known for their stability and low loss, making them suitable for precision applications. X7R capacitors provide a good balance of capacitance, voltage, and stability, ideal for general usage. Y5V and Z5U capacitors have higher capacitance values but exhibit significant changes in capacitance with temperature and voltage, making them suitable for less critical applications. Other types may include specialized dielectrics for unique applications.</p></p>
