<p><p>The wireless wearable speaker market encompasses two primary types based on power sources: built-in battery and external battery systems. Built-in battery speakers are compact, integrating rechargeable batteries within the device for convenience and portability. These are ideal for users seeking an all-in-one solution. Conversely, external battery speakers rely on detachable power sources, allowing for extended usage through the replacement or recharging of separate batteries. This type offers flexibility for users who need longer playtime without worrying about charging the device frequently.</p></p>
