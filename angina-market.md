<p><p>The angina market comprises various drug classes targeting different mechanisms to alleviate symptoms and improve cardiac function. Nitrates relieve anginal pain by dilating coronary arteries. ACE inhibitors lower blood pressure and reduce strain on the heart, while ranolazine offers metabolic benefits by improving myocardial energy metabolism. Each class plays a distinct role in managing angina effectively.</p></p>
