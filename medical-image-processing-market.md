<p><p>The medical image processing market encompasses various imaging modalities, each serving distinct diagnostic purposes. CT-Scans provide detailed cross-sectional images for internal examination, while X-rays offer quick, basic evaluations of bone structures. Ultrasound uses sound waves for real-time imaging of soft tissues, and MRI delivers high-resolution images through magnetic fields, ideal for soft tissue assessment. Nuclear imaging visualizes physiological functions using radioactive tracers, while the “Others” category includes emerging technologies and hybrid systems enhancing diagnostic capabilities. This diversity caters to various clinical needs.</p></p>
