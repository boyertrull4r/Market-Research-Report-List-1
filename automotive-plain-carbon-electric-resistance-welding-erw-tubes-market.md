<p><p>The Automotive Plain Carbon Electric Resistance Welding (ERW) Tubes market is characterized by two primary types: Straight Seam Welded Pipe and Spiral Welded Pipe. Straight Seam Welded Pipes are produced by joining flat metal strips along a straight line, resulting in high precision and strength, often used in structural applications. Spiral Welded Pipes, on the other hand, are formed by wrapping a metal strip in a spiral, allowing for larger diameters and enhanced flexibility in design, commonly utilized in fluid transportation and automotive components.</p></p>
