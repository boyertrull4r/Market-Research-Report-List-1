<p><ul><li>Heat Sterilization</li><li>Low Temperature Sterilization</li><li>Filtration Sterilization</li><li>Radiation Sterilization</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sterilization equipment and disinfectants market comprises various types, each suited for specific applications. Heat sterilization uses high temperatures to eradicate microorganisms, ideal for heat-resistant materials. Low-temperature sterilization employs gases or liquids, suitable for sensitive instruments. Filtration sterilization utilizes physical barriers to remove pathogens from liquids and gases, making it effective for heat-sensitive solutions. Radiation sterilization uses ionizing radiation to disinfect materials without heat, often applied in the medical and pharmaceutical industries for ensuring product safety.</p></p>
