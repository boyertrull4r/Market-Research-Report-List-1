<p><p>The marine vessels fuel cell market is primarily divided into two types: Polymer Electrolyte Membrane Fuel Cells (PEMFC) and Solid Oxide Fuel Cells (SOFC). PEMFCs are favored for their quick start-up capabilities and efficiency at lower temperatures, suitable for smaller vessels and auxiliary power. In contrast, SOFCs operate at higher temperatures, offering higher efficiency and long-term durability, making them ideal for larger marine applications and continuous power generation. Both technologies aim to reduce emissions and promote cleaner marine operations.</p></p>
