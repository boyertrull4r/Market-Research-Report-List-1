<p><p>The Coefficient of Friction Tester market is categorized into two main types: Static Coefficient of Friction and Kinetic Coefficient of Friction. The Static Coefficient of Friction measures the resistance to the initial movement between two surfaces, crucial for determining the grip and stability of materials. Conversely, the Kinetic Coefficient of Friction evaluates the friction encountered when surfaces slide against each other, reflecting the performance in dynamic scenarios. Both types are essential for industries requiring material compatibility and performance assessments.</p></p>
