<p><p>Fiber pumped laser modules are categorized into two main types: single module and multi-module systems. Single module systems consist of a single pump laser unit, offering a compact and cost-effective solution for applications needing moderate output power and simplicity. In contrast, multi-module systems integrate multiple laser units, enhancing output power, reliability, and scalability. This allows for greater versatility in demanding applications, such as industrial processing and medical uses, where higher intensity and performance are essential.</p></p>
