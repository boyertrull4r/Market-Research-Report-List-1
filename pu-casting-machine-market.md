<p><strong>The PU Casting Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>High Pressure</li><li>Low Pressure</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PU casting machine market can be categorized into high pressure and low pressure types based on the pressure applied during the polyurethane casting process. High-pressure machines utilize elevated pressure to ensure precise mixing and rapid curing of polyurethane, resulting in highly detailed and intricate parts, often used in automotive and aerospace applications. In contrast, low-pressure machines operate at reduced pressure, allowing for easier processing of larger components and reducing air entrapment, making them ideal for less complex molds in various industries.</p></p>
