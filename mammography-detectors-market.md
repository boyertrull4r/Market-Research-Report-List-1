<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Mammography Detectors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Computed Radiography Detectors</li><li>Flat Panel Detectors</li><li>CMOS Flat Detectors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mammography detectors market comprises three main types: Computed Radiography (CR) Detectors, Flat Panel Detectors, and CMOS Flat Detectors. CR Detectors use phosphor plates to capture images, then convert them digitally, offering flexibility and cost-effectiveness. Flat Panel Detectors utilize direct or indirect conversion of X-rays into digital signals, providing high image quality and faster processing. CMOS Flat Detectors use complementary metal-oxide-semiconductor technology for high-resolution imaging and low power consumption, enhancing diagnostic accuracy and workflow efficiency in mammography.</p></p>
