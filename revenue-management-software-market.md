<p><p>Revenue Management Software (RMS) can be categorized into two main types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based RMS operates over the internet, offering flexibility, scalability, and lower upfront costs, making it accessible for businesses of all sizes. It supports real-time data access and collaboration. In contrast, on-premises RMS is installed locally on a company’s servers, providing greater control over data security and customization but often requiring higher initial investment and maintenance, appealing to organizations with specific needs or regulatory concerns.</p></p>
