<p><ul><li>Permanent Magnet</li><li>Electromagnetic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Magnetic Sheet Separators market comprises two main types: Permanent Magnet and Electromagnetic separators. Permanent Magnet separators utilize static magnetic fields generated by magnets, making them energy-efficient and low-maintenance options for material handling in various industries. In contrast, Electromagnetic separators employ electric currents to produce variable magnetic fields, offering flexibility and control for specific applications. Both types are crucial for enhancing productivity by efficiently sorting and separating ferrous materials from other substances in manufacturing and recycling processes.</p></p>
