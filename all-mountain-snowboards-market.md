<p><strong>The All-Mountain Snowboards Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>True Twin</li><li>Directional Twin</li><li>Tapered</li></ul></p>
<p><p>All-Mountain snowboards are categorized into three main types: True Twin, Directional Twin, and Tapered. True Twin boards feature symmetrical shapes for balanced riding in both directions, perfect for freestyle and park use. Directional Twin boards are slightly asymmetrical, offering enhanced performance in one direction while still allowing for switch riding. Tapered boards have a narrower tail and wider nose, providing excellent float in powder and stability in varied terrains, making them ideal for backcountry and off-piste adventures.</p></p>
