<p><strong>The Automotive Active Roll Control System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electric Active Roll Control (EARC)</li><li>Hydraulic Active Roll Control (HARC)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Active Roll Control System market comprises two primary types: Electric Active Roll Control (EARC) and Hydraulic Active Roll Control (HARC). EARC utilizes electric motors and sensors to actively manage vehicle stability and minimize body roll during cornering by adjusting suspension stiffness. In contrast, HARC employs hydraulic actuators, using pressurized fluid to control roll dynamics and improve vehicle handling. Both systems enhance safety and ride comfort, catering to the growing demand for advanced vehicle stability technologies in modern automotive design.</p></p>
