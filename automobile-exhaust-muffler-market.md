<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Automobile Exhaust Muffler Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Center Inlet</li><li>Dual Inlet</li><li>Offset Inlet</li><li>Center Outlet</li><li>Dual Outlet</li><li>Offset Outlet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automobile exhaust muffler market includes various types based on inlet and outlet configurations. **Center Inlet** mufflers have a single inlet at the center for efficient flow. **Dual Inlet** designs accommodate two inlets for enhanced performance. **Offset Inlet** mufflers feature an inlet positioned off-center, optimizing installation space. **Center Outlet** models ensure balanced exhaust flow, while **Dual Outlet** types provide increased power by offering two paths for exhaust gases. Lastly, **Offset Outlet** mufflers position the outlet off-center, helping with vehicle design and performance tuning.</p></p>
