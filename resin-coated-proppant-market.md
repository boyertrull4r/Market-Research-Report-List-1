<p><ul><li>Curable Resin-Coated Proppant</li><li>Precured Resin-Coated Proppant</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The resin-coated proppant market is divided into two primary types: curable resin-coated proppant and precured resin-coated proppant. Curable resin-coated proppant is coated with a resin that hardens during the curing process, enhancing its performance under high-pressure conditions. In contrast, precured resin-coated proppant has already undergone the curing process before application, offering immediate strength and stability. Both types are designed to improve well productivity and reduce downhole damage, making them essential in hydraulic fracturing operations within the oil and gas industry.</p></p>
