<p><strong>The Conventional Fire Detectors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Heat Detector Type</li><li>Ionization Detector Type</li><li>Photoelectric Detector Type</li><li>Ionization/Photoelectric Detector Type</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The conventional fire detectors market encompasses various types designed to detect smoke and heat. Heat detectors respond to temperature changes, suitable for areas with dust or steam. Ionization detectors use radioactive material to sense fast-burning fires by detecting ionized particles. Photoelectric detectors are sensitive to smoldering fires, using light beams to identify smoke. Ionization/photoelectric detectors combine both technologies for broader detection capabilities. Other types include combination detectors and specialty detectors tailored for specific environments, enhancing overall fire safety.</p></p>
