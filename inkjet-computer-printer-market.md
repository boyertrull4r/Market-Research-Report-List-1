<p><p>The inkjet printer market comprises several technologies. Continuous Inkjet technology involves a constant flow of ink, allowing for high-speed printing, ideal for industrial applications. Thermal Bubble Inkjet Technology uses heat to create bubbles that propel ink onto paper, providing good quality for home and office use. Micro Piezo Technology employs piezoelectric elements to control ink droplets, ensuring precision and versatility in printing. Additionally, the Other Inks market includes various specialized inks for unique applications, catering to diverse consumer needs in the printing industry.</p></p>
