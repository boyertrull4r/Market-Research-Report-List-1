<p><p>The double pipe shell and tube heat exchanger market includes several key types: </p><p>1. **Fixed-Tube Sheet Exchanger** features fixed tube sheets, providing a simple design but limited thermal expansion.</p><p>2. **U-Tube Exchanger** has tubes bent in a U-shape, allowing for thermal expansion and easy maintenance while providing effective heat transfer.</p><p>3. **Floating-Head Exchanger** accommodates movement between the shell and tube bundles, ideal for high-temperature applications, enabling easy cleaning and maintenance while minimizing thermal stress.</p></p>
