<p><ul><li>A Type</li><li>X Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Molecular sieve raw powders are classified primarily into A Type and X Type based on their pore structure and size. A Type molecular sieves are characterized by their uniform pores of approximately 3 angstroms, allowing selective adsorption of smaller molecules, such as water and nitrogen. X Type molecular sieves, with larger pores of about 10 angstroms, can capture larger molecules, including hydrocarbons. These distinctions make each type suitable for specific applications in industries like petrochemicals, pharmaceuticals, and drying agents.</p></p>
