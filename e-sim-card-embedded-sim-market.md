<p><p>E-SIM cards, or Embedded SIMs, are integrated into devices, eliminating the need for a physical SIM card. The market for E-SIMs is categorized into two types: SMD (Surface Mount Device) and SIP (System in Package). SMD E-SIMs are soldered onto the device's motherboard, offering robustness and compactness. Conversely, SIP E-SIMs combine multiple components into a single package, allowing for greater integration and efficiency. Both types enhance connectivity options and facilitate remote SIM management for end-users.</p></p>
