<p><p>The tea pods market consists of two main types: soft tea pods and hard tea pods. Soft tea pods are made from biodegradable materials and are typically used for single-serve brewing, offering a fresher taste and quick preparation. Hard tea pods, on the other hand, are created from sturdy materials like plastic or aluminum, providing longer shelf life and easier handling, but they may require more complex brewing machines. Both types cater to growing consumer demand for convenience and quality in tea preparation.</p></p>
