<p><p>The high-density calcined coke market consists of several types, including needle coke, shot coke, sponge coke, and honeycomb coke. Needle coke is characterized by its elongated structure, making it ideal for producing electrodes in electric arc furnaces. Shot coke features a spherical shape, used primarily in aluminum production. Sponge coke has a more porous structure, utilized in various applications like anode production. Honeycomb coke, known for its unique shape, is commonly used in specialized applications, enhancing overall performance in industrial processes.</p></p>
