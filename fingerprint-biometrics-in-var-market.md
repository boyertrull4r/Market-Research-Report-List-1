<p><p>Fingerprint biometrics in the VAR market can be categorized into two main types: Non-AFIS and AFIS technology. Non-AFIS technology refers to standalone fingerprint recognition systems used for access control or identity verification, typically in smaller applications. In contrast, AFIS (Automated Fingerprint Identification System) technology is a comprehensive network that processes and matches large volumes of fingerprint data, often used by law enforcement and large institutions for criminal identification and background checks. Both systems enhance security and streamline identification processes.</p></p>
