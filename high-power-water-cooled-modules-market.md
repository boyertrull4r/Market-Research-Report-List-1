<p><strong>The High Power Water Cooled Modules Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dual Diode</li><li>Thyristor-Diode</li><li>Thyristor-Thyristor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>High Power Water Cooled Modules are categorized into three main types: Dual Diode, Thyristor-Diode, and Thyristor-Thyristor. Dual Diode modules utilize two diodes for efficient rectification, ideal for applications requiring high reliability. Thyristor-Diode modules combine a thyristor with a diode, allowing for precise control over power delivery and enabling rapid switching applications. Thyristor-Thyristor modules use two thyristors, offering robust control for high-power applications, particularly in industrial settings where efficiency and thermal management are critical.</p></p>
