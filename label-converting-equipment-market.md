<p><p>Label converting equipment can be categorized into three main types: manual, semi-automatic, and automatic. Manual equipment requires operators to perform labeling tasks by hand, making it suitable for low-volume applications. Semi-automatic machines streamline the process by allowing operators to assist in feeding and positioning labels while the equipment handles the dispensing. Automatic label converters fully automate the labeling process, minimizing human intervention and enhancing production speed and efficiency, making them ideal for high-volume production environments.</p></p>
