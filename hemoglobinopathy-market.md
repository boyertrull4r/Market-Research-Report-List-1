<p><p>The hemoglobinopathy market comprises three main types: Sickle Cell Disease, Alpha Thalassemia, and Beta Thalassemia. Sickle Cell Disease is characterized by abnormal hemoglobin leading to distorted red blood cells, causing pain and complications. Alpha Thalassemia involves the reduced production of alpha globin chains, affecting oxygen transport and leading to anemia. Beta Thalassemia results from decreased beta globin production, also causing anemia and requiring regular blood transfusions. Each type presents unique treatment challenges, influencing market dynamics and opportunities.</p></p>
