<p><p>The artillery system market is categorized into three main types based on caliber size: small caliber, medium caliber, and heavy caliber. Small caliber systems, typically below 20mm, are used for rapid fire and close-range support. Medium caliber systems, ranging from 20mm to 105mm, provide a balance of firepower and mobility for diverse combat scenarios. Heavy caliber systems, exceeding 105mm, deliver significant firepower for long-range strikes, often utilized in strategic military operations and defense applications. Each category serves distinct tactical roles in modern warfare.</p></p>
