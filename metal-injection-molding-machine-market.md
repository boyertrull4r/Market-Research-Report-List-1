<p><p>Metal Injection Molding (MIM) machines are specialized equipment used to produce complex metal parts through a process that combines the advantages of traditional powder metallurgy with injection molding. MIM involves mixing fine metal powders with binding materials to create a feedstock that is then injected into molds. This technique allows for high-volume production of intricate shapes that may be difficult or costly to achieve using conventional machining methods.</p><p>The Metal Injection Molding Machine Market is anticipated to grow at a CAGR of 6.6% during the forecast period.
