<p><p>The automotive tire tread sensors market primarily includes tire integrated sensors and standalone tire tread sensors. Tire integrated sensors are embedded within the tire structure, providing real-time data on tire pressure, temperature, and tread wear directly from the tire. Standalone tire tread sensors, however, are separate devices that are used to measure tread depth and assess tire condition. Both types enhance vehicle safety and efficiency by promoting timely maintenance and ensuring optimal tire performance, ultimately contributing to improved road safety.</p></p>
