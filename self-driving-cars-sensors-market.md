<p><p>The self-driving cars sensors market is primarily categorized into three key types: camera sensors, radar sensors, and lidar sensors. Camera sensors utilize advanced imaging technology to perceive the vehicle's surroundings, helping with object detection and traffic sign recognition. Radar sensors, employing radio waves, excel in measuring distances and detecting objects in various weather conditions. Lidar sensors use laser pulses to create precise 3D maps of the environment, enhancing navigation and obstacle avoidance. Each sensor type contributes uniquely to the safe functioning of autonomous vehicles.</p></p>
