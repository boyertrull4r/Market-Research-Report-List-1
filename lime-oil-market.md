<p><strong>The Lime Oil Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Distilled</li><li>Expressed</li><li>Essence</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The lime oil market is categorized into three types: distilled, expressed, and essence. Distilled lime oil is obtained through steam distillation, capturing the essence of the fruit in a concentrated form. Expressed lime oil, also known as cold-pressed oil, is extracted by mechanically pressing the rind, retaining the vibrant aroma and flavor. Essence, on the other hand, refers to a synthetic or concentrated version that mimics lime oil characteristics, often used in flavoring and perfumes. Each type serves distinct applications across industries.</p></p>
