<p><p>The Fatigue Driving Monitoring System market comprises two primary types: Sensor Monitoring Systems and Camera Monitoring Systems. Sensor Monitoring Systems utilize various sensors to detect physiological signs of drowsiness, such as eye closure and head movement, alerting drivers when fatigue is detected. In contrast, Camera Monitoring Systems employ advanced imaging technology to monitor driver behavior and facial expressions, identifying signs of fatigue visually. Both systems aim to enhance road safety by preventing accidents caused by driver fatigue.</p></p>
