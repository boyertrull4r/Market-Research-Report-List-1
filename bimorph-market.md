<p><strong>The Bimorph Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Piezoelectric Bimorph</li><li>Bimetal Bimorph</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bimorph market comprises two main types: piezoelectric bimorphs and bimetal bimorphs. Piezoelectric bimorphs utilize piezoelectric materials that generate voltage when deformed, making them ideal for applications in sensors, actuators, and energy harvesting. In contrast, bimetal bimorphs consist of two different metals bonded together, which bend when heated due to differing thermal expansion rates, commonly used in thermostats and temperature sensors. Both types cater to various industrial needs, enhancing device functionality and performance.</p></p>
