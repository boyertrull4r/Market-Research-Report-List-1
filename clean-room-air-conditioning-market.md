<p><p>The Clean Room Air Conditioning market is categorized into decentralized and centralized systems. Decentralized systems consist of individual units that directly control air quality within specific rooms, offering flexibility and easier maintenance. In contrast, centralized systems manage air quality through a central unit that distributes conditioned air to multiple rooms, providing uniform environmental control and efficiency for larger facilities. Both types are crucial for maintaining stringent cleanliness standards in industries like pharmaceuticals, electronics, and biotechnology.</p></p>
