<p><p>Ultrasonic cleansers are devices that use high-frequency sound waves, typically above 20 kHz, to agitate a fluid, creating cavitation bubbles. These bubbles implode with great force, producing micro-jets that dislodge contaminants from surfaces of objects submerged in the cleaning solution. This technology is particularly effective for delicate items such as jewelry, lenses, and industrial components, where traditional cleaning methods might be abrasive or inefficient.</p><p>The Ultrasonic Cleanser Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.8% during the forecast period.
