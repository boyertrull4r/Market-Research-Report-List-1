<p><p>Magnetic flowmeters, also known as magmeters, are widely used in various industries to measure the flow of conductive liquids. They operate on the principle of Faraday's law of electromagnetic induction, where a magnetic field is applied to a conductive liquid, generating a voltage proportional to the flow rate. This method offers high accuracy, low maintenance, and is ideal for applications involving wastewater, chemicals, and food processing.</p><p>The Magnetic Flowmeters Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.5% during the forecast period.
