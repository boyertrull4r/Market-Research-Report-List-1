<p><ul><li>Fat Turpentine</li><li>Sulphate Turpentine</li><li>Wood Turpentine</li><li>Carbonization Turpentine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The turpentine oil market comprises several types, including Fat Turpentine, Sulphate Turpentine, Wood Turpentine, and Carbonization Turpentine. Fat Turpentine is extracted from pine resin and used in paint and varnish production. Sulphate Turpentine, a byproduct of the kraft pulping process, is utilized in chemical manufacturing. Wood Turpentine is derived directly from the distillation of wood and finds applications in solvents and flavorings. Carbonization Turpentine is obtained from carbonizing wood or biomass, serving niche applications in specialty chemicals.</p></p>
