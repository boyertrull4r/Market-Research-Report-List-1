<p><p>The Reverse Osmosis Thin Film market comprises two primary types: cellulose thin film and composite thin film. Cellulose thin film membranes are derived from natural polymers, offering good permeability and moderate rejection rates, making them suitable for various water purification applications. In contrast, composite thin film membranes utilize several layers of materials to enhance performance, providing superior rejection of contaminants and increased durability. Together, these technologies cater to diverse industrial and municipal water treatment needs, each with its specific advantages.</p></p>
